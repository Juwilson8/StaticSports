What is Static Sports?

Static Sports strives to maximize coaches' and athletes' performance by simplifying game planning and in-game decision-making through the use of data analytics

  • Football

    Static sports is both beneficial to athletes and coaches. Our software allows coaches to simplify in game decision making via charting plays throughout a game. Static sports helps athletes with gameplanning by, helping them learn how to watch film, understand offensive or defensive formations/concepts and recognize opponents strengths and weaknesses.
