Athletes

Static sports is an effective tool for enhancing athletes' comprehension of the game of football. By using this platform, athletes can gain a better understanding of both offensive and defensive formations, as well as personnel. They can also learn how to identify potential plays that can be executed from specific formations and personnel groups.

Furthermore, athletes can improve their awareness of play concepts that are commonly used in different situations, such as down and distance. By utilizing static sports, athletes can also gain a better understanding of their team's playbook at all levels and its objectives. They can learn how to determine which play call would be most effective in countering the opposing team's play.

For high school athletes, using static sports can give them a competitive edge and prepare them for college football. By enhancing their knowledge of the game, they can stay ahead of their peers and improve their chances of success at the next level.

Coach's

Static sports can serve multiple purposes for coaches. It can be utilized as a valuable tool for game planning and decision making during games.

By analyzing film on their upcoming opponents, coaches can utilize static sports to calculate their opponents' frequently used concepts, formations, personnel groups, route concepts, gap schemes, defensive fronts, and coverages.

In addition, coaches can utilize the game planning feature of static sports to aid their athletes in preparing for upcoming games. The sortable table provided by static sports can also assist coaches with play calling during games.
